About

The Pakistan Oilfields Limited (POL) Hospital in Khaur, Punjab, is a well-established medical facility that primarily serves the employees of POL and the local community. It was established to provide quality healthcare services in the region, catering to a wide range of medical needs. The hospital is equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed by qualified healthcare professionals, ensuring comprehensive medical care for patients. Over the years, the POL Hospital has played a vital role in improving healthcare access in Khaur and surrounding areas.
